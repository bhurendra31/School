CURRICULUM


In the early years, we focus on experiential learning through play. Our Play Group and Pre-Primary programs emphasize social development, creativity, and the joy of learning. Through interactive activities, games, and storytelling, children begin their educational journey in a nurturing and stimulating environment.



APPROACH TO LEARNING


  • Student-centered approach to education.
  • Dynamic and interactive teaching methods.
  • Tailored to individual learning styles.
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention.
  • Integration of technology into classrooms for digital preparedness.
  • Emphasis on both academic achievement and the learning process.




PRIMARY LEVEL (GRADES 1-5)


  • Focus on early childhood development.
  • Emphasis on play-based learning, fostering creativity, social skills, and emotional development.
  • Introduction to basic concepts in literacy (letters, phonics, early reading), numeracy (numbers, counting, shapes), and motor skills (drawing, coloring, cutting).
  • Activities designed to develop communication and language skills through songs, storytelling, and interactive sessions.
  • Learning through sensory play, puzzles, and games that build problem-solving and critical thinking abilities.






LOWER SECONDARY (GRADES 6-8)


  • Language Development: Strong foundation in English and Nepali language, focusing on reading, writing, comprehension, and speaking.
  • Mathematics: Basic concepts including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, along with geometry and measurement.
  • Science: Introduction to nature, plants, animals, and basic scientific principles to build curiosity.
  • Social Studies: Learning about community, geography, and cultural heritage to promote awareness of society and environment.
  • Creative Development: Emphasis on arts, music, and physical education to develop creativity and encourage physical well-being.
  • Moral and Ethical Learning: Lessons on values such as respect, teamwork, and responsibility.







Secondary Level (Grades 9-10)


  • English and Nepali: Advanced study in literature, essay writing, debates, and critical analysis of texts.
  • Mathematics: In-depth exploration of algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and statistics.
  • Science: Comprehensive study in physics, chemistry, and biology, with practical lab experiments to enhance understanding.
  • Social Studies: Focus on national history, economics, geography, and global politics.
  • Computer Science: Advanced concepts in coding, data processing, and the use of software applications.
  • Physical Education: Continued participation in sports and physical activities to promote a healthy lifestyle.
  • Vocational and Life Skills: Introduction to life skills, including financial literacy, problem-solving, and leadership training.
  • Arts and Music: Continued development of creative skills, offering students opportunities to engage in music, theatre, and visual arts.

This curriculum ensures a holistic development of students, preparing them for future academic challenges and personal growth, while fostering skills necessary for success in a rapidly changing world.
