ABOUT

Himali English Secondary Boarding School Located in the heart of JanakpurDham, at Kadam Chowk, Dhanusha, Nepal, Himali English Secondary Boarding School has been a pillar of educational excellence since its establishment in 2049 BS. As a private, boarding institution, we provide a holistic K-12 educational experience, catering to students from elementary through high school levels. Our commitment to nurturing academic and personal growth in a supportive environment has made us one of the region’s most reputable educational institutions. MISSION

At Himali English Secondary Boarding School, our mission is to deliver high-quality education that empowers students to reach their full potential. We are dedicated to fostering an environment of academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

VISION

Our vision is to create a bright future for our students, equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and values they need to succeed in a rapidly changing world. We aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who will make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

INFRASTRUCTURE

The infrastructure of Himali English Secondary Boarding School is designed to support holistic education, featuring modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, and ample recreational facilities.



Modern Classrooms

Modern classrooms at Himali English Secondary Boarding School are designed to create an engaging and conducive learning environment. Each classroom is spacious, well-lit, and equipped with the latest teaching aids, including projectors and smart boards. The ergonomic seating ensures students’ comfort, fostering better concentration and participation. Interactive learning is encouraged through technology integration, making lessons dynamic and more impactful.

Physics Lab

SThe Physics Lab at Himali English Secondary Boarding School is fully equipped with modern instruments to facilitate hands-on learning and experimentation. Students have access to various apparatus such as oscilloscopes, lenses, prisms, and electrical circuits to explore fundamental principles of physics. The lab is designed to provide a safe and interactive environment where students can conduct experiments related to mechanics, optics, electromagnetism, and thermodynamics. Experienced instructors guide students through practical applications of theoretical concepts, fostering a deeper understanding of the subject.

Computer Lab

The computer lab at Himali English Secondary Boarding School is equipped with the latest hardware and software to facilitate effective learning in technology. Students have access to high-speed internet and modern computers, enabling them to engage in coding, research, and digital projects. The lab is designed to support IT education and practical applications, ensuring students are well-prepared for the digital age. Regular maintenance and updates are performed to keep the technology current.

Library

The library at Himali English Secondary Boarding School is a hub of knowledge, offering a diverse collection of books, reference materials, and digital resources. It provides students with access to academic texts, literature, and journals to support their studies and personal growth. Equipped with comfortable seating and a quiet ambiance, the library encourages a culture of reading and research. It also integrates modern technology, allowing students to access e-books and online databases for broader learning opportunities.

Playground

The playground at Himali English Secondary Boarding School offers a large, open space designed for physical activities and sports. It provides facilities for games like football, basketball, and volleyball, encouraging students to stay active and develop teamwork skills. The well-maintained area promotes a healthy, outdoor environment for recreation and relaxation. Students of all ages can engage in both organized sports and free play, enhancing their physical fitness and social interaction.

Art and Music Rooms

The Art and Music Rooms at Himali English Secondary Boarding School are designed to foster creativity and artistic expression among students. These rooms are equipped with various instruments, art supplies, and tools to support both music and visual arts education. Students can explore their talents in painting, sculpture, vocal training, and instrumental music under the guidance of experienced instructors. The spaces provide a nurturing environment for creative growth and cultural appreciation.

Chemistry Lab

The chemistry lab at Himali English Secondary Boarding School is equipped with modern apparatus and safety equipment, providing students with a hands-on learning environment. It allows for practical experiments that reinforce theoretical knowledge, fostering a deeper understanding of chemical reactions and principles. The lab is designed to accommodate group activities, encouraging collaboration among students. Safety measures are in place to ensure a secure and educational experience for all learners.
