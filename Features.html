TRANSPORTATION

Himali English Boa. School provides van/bus facilities that are available on selected routes. The details of routes, bus timings and conveyance charges may be obtained by the parents directly from the Transport Supervisor.

  • The student shall be at the bus stop at least 10 minutes before the scheduled pick-up time.
  • In case of missing the bus, students shall have to make their own arrangements to reach school.
  • A change in the approved route and door-to-door pickup will not be provided to avoid delays.
  • If a change in the pickup/drop-off point is to be made, parents must notify the transport supervisor well in advance via a written note.
  • If a different travel arrangement has been made for a day, parents must convey it to the transport supervisor well in advance via a written note.


FIRST AID


  • Basic first aid kits are available in each classroom and at key locations throughout the school.
  • Trained staff members are available to provide immediate care for minor injuries and illnesses.
  • First aid supplies are regularly checked and restocked to ensure availability.
  • Designated staff members are trained in CPR and emergency response techniques.
  • A sick room is available for students to rest if they feel unwell during school hours.
  • For more serious injuries or emergencies, parents are notified promptly, and professional medical assistance is sought as needed.
  • Emergency contact information for each student is kept up to date and easily accessible.
  • Regular health and safety drills are conducted to ensure staff and students are prepared for emergencies.




SCHOOL LIBRARY


Our school library is one of the many educational resources we provide for the benefit of our students.
Students must observe strict silence in the library.

  • Books will be issued to the students only during regular library periods.
  • Students are allowed to borrow books only with their own identity card.
  • Books will be issued for a duration of one week only.
  • Students are expected to take proper care of the books they borrow.
  • Lost, marked, damaged, or disfigured books must be replaced or paid for by the borrower.
  • Before borrowing a new book, the previously borrowed book must be returned.
  • Books must be returned before long holidays or vacations commence.
  • A fine will be levied if the book is not returned within the scheduled time.
  • All articles and personal belongings must be left outside while entering the library.
