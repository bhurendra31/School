SCHOOL EVENTS

At Himali English Secondary Boarding School, we believe in the holistic development of our students by nurturing not only academic excellence but also creativity, teamwork, and leadership through various events and activities. Our school regularly organizes a wide array of events that enrich the student experience, foster community spirit, and provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents and skills.

ANNUAL SPORTS DAY

The Annual Sports Day is a highly anticipated event where students from all grade levels participate in athletic competitions, including races, team sports, and fun activities. This event promotes physical fitness, sportsmanship, and team spirit.

CULTURAL PROGRAM

Our Cultural Program celebrates the diverse traditions and cultures represented in our school community. Students perform dances, songs, and dramas that highlight Nepal’s rich cultural heritage, while also celebrating global cultures.

SCIENCE EXHIBITION

Organized annually, the Science Exhibition allows students to present their innovative projects and experiments. This event encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity, as students demonstrate their scientific knowledge in fields such as physics, chemistry, and biology.

ART & MUSIC SHOW

Showcasing the artistic talents of our students, the Art and Music Show is an event where students display their creative works, including paintings, sculptures, and musical performances. This event highlights the importance of the arts in education.

DEBATE & QUIZ COMPETITIONS

To enhance students’ critical thinking and communication skills, we regularly organize debate and quiz competitions. These events challenge students to engage in intellectual discussions and test their knowledge across various subjects.

ANNUAL DAY CELEBRATION

The Annual Day Celebration is a grand event where students and faculty come together to celebrate the achievements of the school. It features speeches, award ceremonies, and a variety of performances by students, making it one of the highlights of the academic year.

ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS CAMPAIGN

In line with our commitment to sustainability, our school organizes environmental awareness campaigns, such as tree planting drives, recycling initiatives, and clean-up programs. These events teach students the importance of environmental conservation and their role in protecting the planet.
